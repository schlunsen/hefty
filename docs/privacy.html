Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

Overview

Hefty Scanner ("the App") is a disk usage analyzer for macOS. Your privacy is important to us, and the App is designed with privacy in mind.

Data Collection

Hefty Scanner does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or usage information. The App operates entirely on your local device.

File Access

The App accesses files and folders on your Mac solely to calculate disk usage and display file sizes. All file scanning happens locally on your device. No file names, paths, sizes, or contents are ever sent anywhere.

Analytics & Tracking

The App contains no analytics, no tracking, no telemetry, and no third-party SDKs.

Network

The App makes no network requests. It works entirely offline.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us at github.com/schlunsen/hefty.
